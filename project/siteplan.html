Exotic Animal World - Site Plan

Site Name

Exotic Animal World - I chose this name because it reflects the organization's mission: rescuing and rehabilitating exotic animals from illegal or dangerous situations. It emphasizes the diversity and wonder of the creatures we protect.

Optional Domain Availability: exotic-animal-world.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a hub for raising awareness about exotic animal rescue, showcasing success stories, and sharing educational content. It will also include an interactive map of the center, an "Animal of the Week" spotlight, and a restaurant menu that helps support the organization financially.

Scenarios

  • “What exotic animal is being featured this week?”
  • “Where can I find the map of the rehabilitation center?”
  • “What food options are available at the on-site restaurant?”

Color Schema

These colors are based on the logo:

  • Primary: Deep Jungle Purple (#3A1743) - Headings, highlights
  • Secondary: Golden Yellow (#FFB400) - Buttons, accents
  • Tertiary: Tropical Green (#3C7A57) - Links, hover effects
  • Neutral: Soft Beige (#FAF4E6) - Backgrounds
  • Dark: Charcoal Black (#111111) - Text, footer

Typography

  • Headings: Righteous (bold, rounded, playful)
  • Body: Lato (clean, readable)
